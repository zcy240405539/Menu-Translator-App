## Full Description

AI Menu APP helps you understand restaurant menus in another language. Upload a menu photo, PDF, document, or menu link, choose your source and target language, and get translated dish names with clear descriptions, ingredients, allergen notes, prices when available, and dish images.

Use it when traveling, exploring a new restaurant, or ordering from a menu that is hard to read. The app can organize dishes by menu category, explain unfamiliar ingredients, and help you compare options before you order.

Signed-in users can keep menu history and save items to an order list for easier review across sessions.
